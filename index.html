Election Intelligence Dashboard

Sidebar

(Beta Version 1.0)

The Election Intelligence dashboard provides up-to-date information regarding:

  • Global national election schedule, changes, and basic information
  • Global forecasted risk of election related violence
  • Forecasted risk of snap election announcements each month for parliamentary systems

This pilot dashboard is based on the Rulers, Elections, and Irregular Governance Dataset (REIGN) and created by the One Earth Future Research forecasting team.
